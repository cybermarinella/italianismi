(page 7 Il lessico della gastronomia; 7.1 Geosinonimi e dialettismiculinari; 7.2 Italianismi gastronomici).

Most of the data comes from the project Dizionario di italianismi nel mondo,
edited by Luca Serianni, Lucilla Pizzoli and Leonardo Rossi between 2006 and 2008.

The data on Italianisms in gastronomy, collected by Lucilla Pizzoli are updated but not necessarily definitive. The languages presented here are only a sample of those included in the original project, which involved the work of dozens of informers. The project is currently being revised and could become part of a new study supported by the Accademia della Crusca.
The data relates only to the meanings regarding the field of gastronomy.
Data sources were mainly lexicographical (in some cases they were based on recent introductions via oral use or restaurant menus).
